Julian Photo Gallery

If you would like a print of any of these photos, please email me the photo number and the size you want. I will be making 5x7s matted to 8x10 and 8x10/8x12 matted to 11x14/12x16, which will all be free of cost if picked up locally in Ithaca. If you want larger prints I'm happy to make them, but due to cost I may have to ask you to cover the cost of the print. If you want something mailed I am also happy to do that, but I will ask you to cover the cost of printing and drop-shipping.

Also please note that these are low-res previews, as it is difficult for me to host lots of large photos. Prints will be made from the high res originals and will in general be significantly sharper. The first seven are my favorites, but I've included all the rest in case something there catches your eye.
